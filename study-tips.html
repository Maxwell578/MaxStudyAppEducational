📚 Smart Study Tips

Level up your focus, learning, and success — one tip at a time!

1. Set SMART Goals 🎯

Break down your big goals into Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-based goals.

2. Use the Pomodoro Technique ⏱️

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat 4 times, then rest longer. Keeps your brain fresh and sharp.

3. Study Actively 🧠

Instead of just reading, write summaries, explain concepts aloud, or teach a friend. You’ll retain more.

4. Create a Distraction-Free Zone 🚫📱

Turn off notifications, clean your desk, and block social media while you’re studying.

5. Review Regularly 🔁

Spacing out your review sessions over days or weeks strengthens your memory much more than cramming.
