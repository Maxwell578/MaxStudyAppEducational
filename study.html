📚 Smart Study Tips

Boost your focus and study like a pro with these powerful tips

⏰Set a Study Schedule

Plan your day and stick to time blocks for studying. Consistency builds results.

📵Minimize Distractions

Turn off notifications, find a quiet space, and use focus tools like Forest or Pomodoro.

📝Take Smart Notes

Use diagrams, bullet points, and highlights. Organize your notes so you learn faster.

🔁Review Regularly

Don't cram! Instead, review your material every few days to help it stick in your mind.

💧Take Breaks & Stay Hydrated

Your brain needs rest. Use 5-10 minute breaks and drink water to refresh your mind.

✅Practice & Test Yourself

Use flashcards, past papers, or quizzes to reinforce learning actively.
